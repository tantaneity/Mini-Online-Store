# Privacy Policy for Mini Online Store

**Last Updated:** November 22, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to Mini Online Store ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our e-commerce platform. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Data

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:
- Register for an account
- Place an order
- Contact customer support
- Subscribe to our newsletter

Personal data we collect includes:
- **Account Information:** Email address, username, password (encrypted)
- **Profile Information:** Name, shipping address, billing address
- **Order Information:** Purchase history, payment information
- **Communication Data:** Support tickets, email correspondence

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you access our application, we automatically collect certain information:
- **Device Information:** IP address, browser type, operating system
- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, time spent on pages, click patterns
- **Cookies and Tracking Technologies:** Session cookies, preference cookies

### 1.3 Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do NOT store complete credit card information
- We do NOT track users across other websites
- We do NOT sell personal data to third parties

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

### 2.1 Service Provision
- Create and manage user accounts
- Process and fulfill orders
- Send order confirmations and updates
- Provide customer support

### 2.2 Business Operations
- Analyze usage patterns to improve our services
- Detect and prevent fraud
- Maintain application security
- Comply with legal obligations

### 2.3 Marketing (Optional)
- Send promotional emails (with your consent)
- Notify you about new products or features
- You can opt-out at any time

## 3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Under GDPR, we process your personal data based on:

- **Contractual Necessity:** To fulfill our contract with you (order processing)
- **Legitimate Interest:** To improve our services and prevent fraud
- **Consent:** For marketing communications (you can withdraw consent anytime)
- **Legal Obligation:** To comply with tax and accounting requirements

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 We Share Data With:

- **Payment Processors:** To process transactions (PCI-DSS compliant)
- **Shipping Providers:** To deliver your orders
- **Analytics Services:** To understand application usage (anonymized data)
- **Legal Authorities:** When required by law or to protect our rights

### 4.2 We Do NOT:
- Sell your personal data to advertisers
- Share data with social media platforms without consent
- Use your data for purposes not disclosed in this policy

## 5. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights:

### 5.1 Right to Access
Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you

### 5.2 Right to Rectification
Correct inaccurate or incomplete information

### 5.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")
Request deletion of your personal data (with some exceptions)

### 5.4 Right to Restrict Processing
Limit how we use your data

### 5.5 Right to Data Portability
Receive your data in a structured, commonly used format

### 5.6 Right to Object
Object to processing based on legitimate interests

### 5.7 Right to Withdraw Consent
Withdraw consent for marketing communications anytime

### 5.8 Right to Lodge a Complaint
File a complaint with your local data protection authority

**To exercise these rights, contact us at:** privacy@ministore.example.com

## 6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

### 6.1 Types of Cookies We Use

| Cookie Type | Purpose | Duration |
|------------|---------|----------|
| **Essential Cookies** | Authentication, security, shopping cart | Session |
| **Preference Cookies** | Remember your settings (language, theme) | 1 year |
| **Analytics Cookies** | Understand usage patterns | 1 year |

### 6.2 Cookie Management

You can control cookies through:
- Our cookie consent banner (when you first visit)
- Your browser settings
- Opt-out of analytics cookies in your account settings

**Note:** Disabling essential cookies may affect application functionality.

## 7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- **Encryption:** All data transmitted using TLS/SSL
- **Password Security:** Passwords hashed using bcrypt
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access to sensitive data
- **Regular Audits:** Security assessments and updates
- **Database Security:** Encrypted at rest, access logging

**However,** no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary:

- **Account Data:** Until you delete your account
- **Order History:** 7 years (for tax and legal compliance)
- **Marketing Consent:** Until you withdraw consent
- **Support Tickets:** 3 years after resolution

After retention periods, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

## 9. Children's Privacy

Our service is NOT intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.
